Home

My hobby is playing a video game called VALORANT

VALORANT is a first person tactical hero shooter created by Riot Games. Players play as one of a set of agents, characters based on serveral countries around the world. Players are assigned to either the attacking or defending team and each team has five players on it.

The main game mode is a 5v5 bomb defuse game mode. The attackers must plant a bomb and let it blow up, defenders must prevent the bomb from exploding.

About Me

Why I like VALORANT

I enjoy VALORANT because there are is a lot of strategy involved and each game involves some amount of critical thinking.

Because there is so many different characters with different abilities and multiple different maps, VALORANT requires a great amount of strategy. There is a rank leaderboard within the game and everytime you win, you climb the leaderboard. I currently sit in the top 10,000 in all of North America.
